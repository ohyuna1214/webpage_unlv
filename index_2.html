Reading Points

  • The Trap of First Impressions: Reflect on the 'Primacy Effect'—how quickly do we judge others based on a single encounter?
  • Focus on Growth: Pay attention to how the characters gradually confront their misunderstandings and change over time. Notice the emotional journey rather than just the outcome.
  • Shifts in Tone and Relationships: Observe how changes in speech, manners, and tone reveal the evolving relationships between characters.
  • Redefining the Tittle: After finishing the novel, ask yourself: Who was truly 'Proud' and who was 'Prejudiced'?
  • Sensory Experience: Try different translations and listen to period-appropriate music to immerse yourself.
